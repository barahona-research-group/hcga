compute_features()[source]¶

Compute the scale free measures of the network.

Scale free calculations using networkx:

Networkx_scale free

The s-metric is defined as the sum of the products deg(u)*deg(v) for every edge (u,v) in G. If norm is provided construct the s-max graph and compute it’s s_metric, and return the normalized s value

1

Lun Li, David Alderson, John C. Doyle, and Walter Willinger, Towards a Theory of Scale-Free Graphs: Definition, Properties, and Implications (Extended Version), 2005. https://arxiv.org/abs/cond-mat/0501169
